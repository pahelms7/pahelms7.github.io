Why It Matters

Two Disciplines, One Provider

Most practices offer chiropractic or physical therapy. Helms Performance offers both, and that changes how your care works. Dr. Helms can address your spine and joints through chiropractic and soft tissue care, then move directly into movement rehabilitation, all in the same visit, with no referral and no change in provider.

Sports Chiropractic

Chiropractic care addresses the spine, joints, and nervous system through manual adjustment, dry needling, and soft tissue techniques. It is particularly effective for structural pain, nerve-related symptoms, and restricted joint mobility. Dr. Helms holds advanced certifications in Active Release Technique and dry needling, expanding what chiropractic care can treat.

Sports Physical Therapy

Physical therapy focuses on movement rehabilitation and functional restoration through exercise therapy, manual techniques, and progressive loading. It is the bridge between pain relief and getting back to full activity, and it builds the strength and coordination that keeps the pain from returning. At Helms Performance, physical therapy is fully integrated into every care plan that calls for it.
