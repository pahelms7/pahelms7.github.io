Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

A few things new patients often ask. If you don't see your question here, send us a message or give us a call.

How quickly will you respond to my message? +

We respond to all form submissions within one business day, often sooner. For a faster response, call us at 301-578-5197 or book online, where you can often find a same-day or next-day slot.

Do I need a referral to book an appointment? +

No referral is needed. Whether you're coming in for sports chiropractic, physical therapy, dry needling, or any of our other services, you can book directly online or by phone. We welcome new patients of all backgrounds and activity levels.

Is Dr. Helms the right fit if I'm not an athlete? +

Absolutely. Dr. Helms works with everyone from competitive runners and weekend golfers to desk workers with neck pain, postpartum mothers, and retirees managing chronic pain. Whether you want to get back to training or just get through your day more comfortably, you'll receive the same one-on-one, individualized care.

What should I bring to my first appointment? +

Wear comfortable clothing you can move in. If you have recent imaging (X-rays or MRI reports) related to your condition, bring those along. If you're planning to use insurance, bring your card. Most importantly, come with a clear sense of what's bothering you and what you'd like to get back to doing that's what shapes everything we do.

Do you accept insurance? +

Please call us at 301-578-5197 to discuss current insurance and payment options. We're happy to answer any questions before your visit so there are no surprises.

Where exactly are you located in the building? +

We're on the second floor of 7625 Wisconsin Ave, Suite 219, Bethesda, MD 20814. There's a parking garage attached to the building. If you have any trouble finding us, call 301-578-5197 and we'll guide you right in.
